The Real Risk

This Isn't IT's Problem. It's Yours.

YOUR ERP

The central nervous system of your supply chain — supplier data, pricing, demand forecasts, payments. One breach takes it all down.

YOUR VENDORS

Tier 2 suppliers connected to your systems with credentials that haven't been rotated since 2019. They get breached, you get breached.

YOUR INTEGRATIONS

Built by people who left. Documented in files nobody can find. Secured by protocols nobody has audited. Every connection is an unlocked door.
