4 Steps to Close the Gap
01 Start with Spend Analytics
Classify and clean your spend data using AI. Most companies have 15-30% of spend that's misclassified or invisible. This is the highest-ROI first step — find savings before you change anything else.
02 Automate Tactical, Focus on Strategic
AI handles PO matching, invoice processing, contract extraction, compliance checking. Humans focus on supplier relationships, negotiation strategy, and category strategy. The goal isn't fewer people — it's people doing higher-value work.
03 Build Predictive Risk Models
Move from reactive to predictive. Monitor supplier financial health, news, geographic risk, and demand signals with AI. Detect bankruptcy indicators months before the force majeure notice arrives.
04 Embed AI in Sourcing Decisions
AI-assisted should-cost models that account for total cost of ownership. Scenario modeling: "If Hormuz stays closed for 6 months, what's our optimal sourcing mix?" Not replacing judgment — augmenting it with better data.
