About the Founder

Two Decades Across the
Full Supply Chain Spectrum

Foley Strategic Advisory was founded by John Foley — a supply chain and procurement executive with over twenty years of experience leading complex operations across consulting, corporate enterprises, and high-stakes government environments.

John has led procurement transformations, built sourcing strategies, designed operating models, and optimized supply chains across multiple industries and geographies. His career spans global management consulting at Accenture, senior procurement leadership at a Fortune 50 corporation managing hundreds of millions in spend, and operational command in environments where failure isn't an option. That range — from boardroom strategy to warehouse floor execution — is what defines FSA's approach.

FSA exists because mid-to-large enterprises deserve a partner who has sat in their chair, managed their complexity, and knows the difference between a strategy that looks good on a slide and one that actually survives contact with reality.
